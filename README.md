## üìä Dashboard Highlights

### 1. **Total Revenue by Month**

* Shows a clear growth trend in total revenue from **January ($27,314)** to **June ($55,083)**.

### 2. **Transaction by Hour of Day**

* Highest activity occurs around **8 AM‚Äì10 AM**, showing peak coffee demand during morning hours.

### 3. **Transaction by Day of Week**

* **Monday and Wednesday** have the highest number of transactions.

### 4. **Transaction by Product Category**

* **Coffee** and **Tea** dominate sales with **20,025** and **16,260 transactions** respectively.
* **Bakery** items are also significant, while niche categories like **Flavours**, **Loose Tea**, and **Packaged Chocolate** have lower sales.

### 5. **Top 15 Products**

* **Brewed Chai Tea** and **Gourmet Brewed Coffee** are top performers, generating the highest revenue.
* Items like **Sugar-free syrup** and **Packaged Chocolate** have low transaction volume and may need review or re-pricing.
